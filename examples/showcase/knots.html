Holotope.js — (p, q) torus knots on the Clifford torus.
The knot lives flat on the Clifford torus in S³ — no pinching, no stretching.
It is the p×q duoprism's vertex path made continuous (toggle the ghost).
At equal spins the double rotation slides the knot along itself.
Drag to orbit · alt-drag to rotate in 4D (xw / yw).
